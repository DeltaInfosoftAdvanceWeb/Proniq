## ‚úÖ Best Practices

### DO:
‚úÖ Use descriptive anchor text with keywords
‚úÖ Link to relevant, related pages
‚úÖ Use natural language in context
‚úÖ Link from high-authority pages to new pages

### DON'T:
‚ùå Use "click here" or "read more"
‚ùå Over-optimize with exact match keywords
‚ùå Link to unrelated pages
‚ùå Create circular linking patterns

---

## üìä Anchor Text Examples

### Good Examples:
- "Learn more about our **workflow-driven ERP platform**"
- "Discover how **RA billing software** can reduce your billing cycle"
- "Integrate with **daily progress tracking** for real-time updates"
- "Our **BOQ management system** prevents excess purchases"

### Bad Examples:
- "Click here to learn more"
- "Read more about this topic"
- "Check out this page"
- "Visit our website"

## üìà Implementation Checklist

For each new page you create:

- [ ] Identify 3-4 related pages to link to
- [ ] Write contextual sentences with natural anchor text
- [ ] Ensure links are relevant to the content
- [ ] Add reciprocal links from target pages back
- [ ] Verify all links work correctly
- [ ] Check that anchor text includes keywords
